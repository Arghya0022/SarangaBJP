About Us

Serving Saranga through organization and public connection.

Saranga BJP Paribar is a local organizational platform for activities, public service, volunteer participation, and communication with supporters.

Our Mission

To build a disciplined and responsive local organization that connects people with public service, awareness programs, and community development efforts.

Our Vision

A stronger Saranga where citizens are informed, connected, and able to participate in constructive local activity.

Party Ideology

National service, good governance, development awareness, social harmony, and local organizational strength.
